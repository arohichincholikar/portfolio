Hi, I’m Arohi!

I’m currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science, majoring in Data Science from GNITS (Hyderabad), but most of my time sits somewhere between coding, designing, organizing creative projects, and endlessly rearranging layouts.

I’ve always been drawn to both the technical and creative sides of technology. I enjoy solving problems through code, but I’m equally interested in how things look, feel, and communicate with people.

Most of my work explores that intersection: websites that are useful and beautiful, data projects that feel engaging, and design work that tells a story.
